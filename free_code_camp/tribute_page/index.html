Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

The Best Man in History – A Mercy to the Worlds

Arabic calligraphy of the name of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ (570 – 632 CE) was the final messenger of Islam, known for his exemplary character, mercy, and justice. His teachings transformed the Arabian Peninsula and continue to guide billions today.

Timeline of His Blessed Life

  • 570 CE – Born in Makkah, known as the Year of the Elephant
  • 576 CE – Lost his mother, becoming an orphan at a young age
  • 582 CE – Traveled to Syria with his uncle Abu Talib
  • 610 CE – Received the first revelation from Angel Jibreel in the Cave of Hira
  • 622 CE – Migrated to Madinah (Hijrah) with his followers
  • 624 CE – Qibla direction changed from Jerusalem to Makkah
  • 632 CE – Delivered the Farewell Sermon during Hajj
  • 632 CE – Passed away in Madinah at the age of 63
“The best among you are those who have the best manners and character.”
— Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
